Trézór Bridge

Trézór Bridge is a communication interface that links your Trézór hardware wallet to web applications, enabling secure interaction without browser extensions. It replaces the older Trézór Chrome extension and provides a more seamless experience.

Key Features

 

  • Secure Connection: Maintains an encrypted channel between your wallet and the app.

  • Cross-Platform Support: Works on Windows, macOS, and Linux.

  • Automatic Updates: Keeps your device communication up to date effortlessly.

 

How to Log In Using Trézór Bridge

 

  • Install Trézór Bridge from the official Trézór website.

  • Connect your Trézór device via USB.

  • Open Trézór Suite or any compatible web app.

  • Follow the prompts on your device to confirm access.

 

Once authenticated, your wallet’s data appears securely on screen—ready for transactions, checking balances, or managing assets.

Logging Out Safely

To log out:

 

  • Disconnect the Trézór device physically.

  • Close the browser session.

  • For added security, clear browser cache and history.

 

Final Thoughts

Trézór Bridge streamlines hardware wallet use, ensuring your digital assets remain protected with minimal friction. Always download it from trusted sources to avoid phishing risks.
